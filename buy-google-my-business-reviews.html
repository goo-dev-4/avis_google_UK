The Complete Guide to Google My Business Reviews

Understanding Google My Business and its impact on your revenue

Google My Business, now officially known as Google Business Profile, is the single most important tool for local businesses in the UK. Your GMB listing appears when customers search for your services on Google Search and Google Maps. The reviews on your GMB profile directly determine whether potential customers choose you or scroll past to a competitor. Businesses with fewer than 10 reviews are often ignored entirely, whilst those with 50 or more reviews dominate their local market.

How GMB reviews influence the Google local pack

The coveted Google local pack -- the top three business listings shown in local search results -- is heavily influenced by your review profile. Google considers three main factors: the total number of reviews, your average star rating, and how recently reviews were posted. By purchasing Google My Business reviews, you send strong signals to Google that your business is active, trusted, and deserving of top placement in local results across the UK.
