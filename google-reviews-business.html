The impact of Google reviews on business growth

Google reviews for business have become one of the most powerful tools for attracting new customers. Studies by BrightLocal reveal that the average consumer reads 10 reviews before feeling able to trust a local business. For companies across the UK, from high-street shops to professional service firms, a strong Google review profile translates directly into increased footfall, phone calls and online enquiries.

How Google reviews influence local search rankings

Google's local search algorithm weighs three primary factors: relevance, distance and prominence. Your business Google reviews play a crucial role in the prominence signal. The more positive, recent reviews your business has, the higher you appear in the local pack and on Google Maps. This means more visibility when potential customers search for businesses like yours in their area.
